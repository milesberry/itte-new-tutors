Books - Education


Capel, S, Leask, M, and Turner, T (2005) Learning to teach in the secondary school (4e) London: RoutledgeFalmer One of the definitive texts for secondary trainees. Easily read and a quote for every assignment. Publisher's website
Cohen, L, Mannion, L, and Morrison, K. (2004) A Guide to Teaching Practice 5th Edition London: RoutledgeFalmer Broader focus than just secondary but has some useful material. Publisher's website
Kyriacou, C. (2007) Essential Teaching Skills 3e Cheltenham: Nelson Thornes
Kyriacou, C. (1997) Effective Teaching in Schools Cheltenham: NelsonThornes		Additional pair of books covering similar areas to Capel et al and Cohen et al. Publisher's website
Muijs, D, and Reynolds, D, (2005) Effective Teaching: Evidence and Practice 2e London: SageConsiders the typical education areas but focuses on the research base behind current views. Provides a good basis for higher order thinking in trainees. Publisher's website

Books - Teaching in Higher Education


Baume, D (2004) Collective Wisdom York: ILTHE Collective wisdom drawn from members who attended the ILTHE Annual Conference 2003. Four sections on assessment, teaching large and small groups and e-learning. Booklet format – very accessible information. Now unavailable since HEA absorbed ILTHE but may be in your library collection.
