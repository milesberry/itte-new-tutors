BBC Learning Zone Broadband is a rich online source of audiovisual material tagged to the school curriculum. Designed for colleges, primary, and secondary schools, Learning Zone Broadband enables teachers to find the best clips to fit lessons. Clips can be searched by subject, topic or keyword and are streamed via the internet. The site is still very much in its infancy but will grow considerably over the next two years to provide thousands of high quality clips for use by teachers in the classroom.Website
